NAME
    SwiftWin.ps1

SYNOPSIS
    Script for swiftly completing many Windows maintenance tasks.

SYNTAX
    SwiftWin.ps1 [-?] [-Debug] [-Verbose] [-Reset] [-Phase {0 | 1 | 2 | 3 | 4}]

DESCRIPTION
    The `SwiftWin.ps1` script runs through a series of Windows 10 maintenance
    tasks I commonly perform when cleaning up and optimizing a system.
